“YAADON KI LEHER”

Aapke jeevan me bhot se chadhav the,
Kya unke upar hamari bhi yaad aati hai?
Jo baatein karne ek dusre se ham, rehte mashroof the,
Kya vo baatien abhi bhi, aapke aalaukik chehre pe muskurahat laati hai?

Aapki yaadein toh hame roze tak rakhwati hai,
Jinka Ramzaan ke baad bhi na ho iftaar,
Par wohi yaadein mann he mann me hasati hai,
Jabh usme aata misaq ka haseen chehra, jiske aage phika har shrungaar.

Agar aapme basta sakaratmak nazariya hai,
Toh hamne bhi jheli hai nakaratmak haqeeqat,
Yadi aisa hoga toh brahmaand ka kanoon hai,
Ki hamare beech jaroor hogi Kashish-e-Barqiyat.

Ek din ham honge ek dusre ke itne kareeb,
Aur khushiyan laut aayengi hamare paas,
Khuda ne hamare khatir farmaya he hai aisa naseeb,
Ki ham naseebwale hai ki hame mil raha, aapke pyaar ka ehsaas.

Aapke liye aasmaan se tare toh nahi,
Par naa kehkar ladkiyo ke dil jaroor todkar laaye hai,
Par mera pyaar itna saccha hai ki tutta toh nahi,
Jabki apne ko bhool jaane ke liye apno se taane khaaye hai.

Jab ham jindagi me ek saath honge,
Toh aapki saare parishaaniya apne sar oothanuga,
Bhale he raaste me kitne bhi kaate honge,
Toh bhi muskurakar unpar se, aapke liye chalkar aaunga.

Har mahine jab aapko dard hoga,
Toh aapke liye shayar se hasya kavi ban jaaunga,
Unn dino aapke haaton me,
Bartan aur zhaadu ki jagah dark chocolate or Teddy bear thamaaunga.

Aapke liye engineer se seedhe bawarchi ban jaaunga,
Aur manpasand khana khilake karaunga roohaani ehsaas,
Aapko Khushi se chain se sulaaunga,
Jaldi se vo din chale bhi jaayenge aur nahi hoga thodusa bhi dard aapke paas.

Aise he haste khelte wakt beetayenge,
Aur dekhenge dilkash aashiqana filmein daale haathon me haath,
Jhagde toh hamare beech hote rahenge,
Par wada karo kabhi na chodenge, ek dusre ka saath.

Aapke liye jitna bhi likhu,
Utna sabh kam hai,
Paar jaate jaate ek shayari aapke khidmat me pesh hai,
Zara tawajjo farmayega, kyuki ham ham hai.

Meri mohabbat se bani duniya ka nazara ho aap,
Meri baaz jaisi nazar ka sitara ho aap,
Meri ishq ki dubi kashti ka kinara ho aap,
Mata pita behen aur do dosto ke baad ek he sahara ho aap,
Kyuki ***** aapka shauhar aur hamari begham Zahra ho aap.

- BY SOMEONE

Meanings:

Mashroof: Busy

Aalukik: Out of this world

Kashish-e-Barqiyat: Electrostatic force of attraction. (sakaratmak=positive charge, nakaratmak= negative charge)

Har mahine ka dard: Menstrual pain

Baawarchi: Chef

Roohani ehsaas: Deep lovely feelings
