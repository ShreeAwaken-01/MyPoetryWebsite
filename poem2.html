JAZBAAT

Meri soch meri kalam toh aap ho ek kitaab,
Aap he meri haqeekat aap he mera khwaab,
Jo muh se keh na paaya vo likh diya jazbaat,
Dil se ki hai mohabbat na dekha dharm jaat.

Meri jaan mai shayar mai likhta shayari,
Karta saccha pyaar na kayar na jhooti aashiqui,
Par iss aashiqui ki samandar me maine kardi badi bhool,
Gum ki rum se bekhudi kardi abh du kaise masool.

Aapki kasam khake kehta na hogi dobaara aise bhool,
Aur aage kabhi bhi, apne main na todunga usool,
Aapka naak hai bhot laazmi na rakhna gussa fizool,
Chahta hu aapke liye teen baar kya, sau baari bolu hai kubool.

Aap na koi vishwasundari aur na toh ho nawab,
Par aapse he itna ishq kyu, iska na hai koi jawaab,
Maana jaan toh jaan se jyada diya aapko maan,
Aap mere sar ka Taj, toh mai aapka Shah Jahaan.

Aap jaise Mata Parvati jo rahi thi khoj,
Jannat si duniya jisme ho keval aanand ka prakaash,
Toh mai banu jaise Mahadev, lene aapke sapno ka bojh,
Aur karu ijaad pyaar ki duniya ka, jaise mahakaay parvat Kailaash.

Aage aane waali pankti bhot he vishesh hai,
Kyonki vo ek shayari, aapki khidmat me pesh hai,
“Vaise toh aapme poore 118 tatvo ka samavesh hai,
Par aap Oganesson ho jisse khuda ne khaas banaya hai;
Aapko pana meri iccha nhi mera aavesh hai,
Isliye toh maine aapka naam, apne dil me chapvaya hai…”

- BY SOMEONE

Meanings:

Bekhudi karna: Hosh kho dena

Masool: Tax (here) fine/penalty

Laazmi: Useful

Fizool: Useless

Mata Parvati wale stanza ka history: Mata Parvati ko ek aise jagah chiye thi jo divine ho and peaceful and happy ho..so Mahadev (Shiv ji / Bholenath) created Kailaash mountain for Mata Parvati.

Last stanza meaning: Ognaesson is the last element so it means U contain all the 118 elements (here…all the required qualities), also that element was made artificially hence its said ki khaas Khuda ne banaya hai , Aavesh: (here) passion.
