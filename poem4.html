CONNOISSEUR OF LOVE

Hello Copper how are you?
I hope you had your food;
But let’s talk later about life,
As right now I’m in poetic mood.

Your memories are like butterflies,
And your love is like a cake:
No matter how much I try,
Your thoughts always keep me awake.

How long will you hide your love,
Not your rejection but your actions reveal your feelings;
You can use a bandage for your cuts,
But the scar in my heart has no such healings.

It has been two years for our love,
Also since I shook hands with any girl with comfort;
And almost one year will pass without us talking,
But “MY LOVE’ you too know, my words weren’t meant to hurt.

Two days before the physics exam,
You thought u would fail;
So I used some motivating yet bitter words,
But u took it in a wrong way and put me in a jail.

Physics was your weak point,
And chemistry was mine;
You aced in the paper,
But our chemistry did not shine.

That jail contains guilt and too much silence,
But still you will only be my special one;
I just want back that license,
And again I will become a bullet and u become my gun.

I know that religion is the only reason,
Because of which you cut this beautiful line;
But for you I can become your favourite season,
And even change my religion to make you mine.

Love like the falling petal,
Is the movie u suggested me to watch;
Without ‘A’ I want the gold metal,
But I must say, your choice is topnotch.

Really you are like a divine power,
Whose gravitation keeps me attracted;
But sometimes my studies lower,
As the image of your smiling face keeps me distracted.

You come in my dreams atleast three times a week,
But there is one unforgettable dream which I cannot miss;
So there too initially you denied that u loved me,
But finally you conveyed it all through a beautiful kiss.

So be ready in next five years I will be at your place,
To ask you out for marriage in front of your dear dad;
I promise that would cause no harm,
And u will surely feel very glad.

Last but not the least, my success would only be my satisfaction,
But my entire happiness lies only in YOU;
I can achieve anything through manifestation,
And I request God that the bride hiding my name in her mehandi
Should be none other than YOU.

After our marriage our emotions will react,
And the product will be our kids, two in fact;
Such that they be the lotion and we their calamine,
And since then forever, we will be each others, special valentine!

- BY SOMEONE

Meanings:

Calamine and lotion: Skin lotion me important compound hota hai calamine.

Special Note:

Everything here is a fact and no stanza is just for the sake of the poem, neither 8th stanza nor 11th and 12th.
