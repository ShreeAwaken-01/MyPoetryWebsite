PYAAR KI NAAV

Aap jaise mere Chand ho,
Aur aapka pyaar jaise Kashish-e-saqal;
Aap jaise mere khushiyo ki naav ho,
Par darta rehta hu kahi ye pyaar na ho safal.

Pyaar ke iss raah me agar aapka saath he na ho,
Toh bhi mai sada rahunga atal;
Aap jaise meri himmat ki naav ho,
Par darta rehta hu kahi ye pyaar na ho safal.

Kya aap hamare liye baadlo me chipkar rote ho,
Aur mohabbat na hone ki karte ho naqal;
Aap jaise mere icchao ki naav ho,
Par darta rehta hu kahi ye pyaar na ho safal.

Sawaal jindagi thi toh aap uske jawaab ho,
Aapke roop me Khuda ne mere liye bheja ek fazal;
Aap jaise mere Astitva ki naav ho,
Par darta rehta hu kahi ye pyaar na ho safal.

- BY SOMEONE

Meanings:

Kashish-e-saqal: Gravitational force of attraction

Baadlon me chipkar rona: The poet has called his lover as moon so it means that when it rains and the moon is behind the clouds so is it you (poet’s lover) who cries while hiding and that you don’t express your feelings for the poet openly.

Fazal: Reward/blessings.
